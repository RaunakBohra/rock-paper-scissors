How to Play


Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple yet exciting game that you can play with a friend or against a computer. The game follows these rules:

  1. Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors)
  2. Scissors beat Paper (Scissors cut Paper)
  3. Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock)

If both players choose the same option, the round is a draw.
